FAQ

What is SEO promotion?

SEO promotion is a set of actions and digital marketing strategies aimed at increasing website traffic and performance through organic results in search engines such as Google, Bing, and even YouTube. New users are attracted through a comprehensive approach that combines SEO, content marketing, and the optimisation of user behaviour signals.

Why is SEO optimisation important?

Search engine optimisation is necessary to improve your website’s positions in search results. Websites on the first results page receive more traffic and have higher conversion rates. This has a positive impact on ROI and the overall growth of the business.

How does SEO optimisation work?

SEO optimisation makes your website more relevant and “attractive” in the eyes of search algorithms. This is achieved through publishing high-quality, relevant content, building a clear structure for pages and sections, increasing website speed, growing the number of quality backlinks, and more.

Who do we work with?

Our core clients are small and medium-sized companies with teams from 5 to 500 people, as well as investors who launch new projects together with our startup studio. These are the partners with whom we achieve the best results. For businesses with limited budgets (up to 5,000 euros or Swiss francs per month), we offer individual training programs tailored to their tasks, followed by consulting sessions instead of a full support team. We are a full-service digital agency working with companies from various industries, except those that face advertising restrictions in the Google networks. We do not work with businesses in gambling, pharmaceuticals, or weapons. Projects related to cryptocurrencies are reviewed on a case-by-case basis.

How do you create a brand that is memorable and makes an impact?

We use a holistic approach that combines in-depth research of your business, market, and target audience with modern analytical methods and proven frameworks. Working closely with you at every stage, we immerse ourselves in your vision, values, and strategic goals. Through clear positioning, unique visual solutions, and a strong message, we help create a cohesive brand identity that stands out in the market and builds lasting emotional loyalty to your brand.

How long does it take to develop a complete brand identity?

The time required depends on the complexity of the project, the scope of work, and your specific needs. Our team of strategists and designers follows a step-by-step methodology that includes research, competitor analysis, concept development, creation of the visual system, and implementation. Typically, developing a strong and consistent brand identity takes from a few weeks to several months. We maintain constant communication with you at every stage to ensure the final result accurately reflects your brand’s essence and supports its long-term success. Exact timelines are determined after we assess your specific goals and requirements.
